How the game works
On each mode page, you’ll enter your name and face a series of rounds. A color word (like “RED”) appears in some font color. Your job is simple:
RULE Press SPACE only when the word and its font color actually match.
• Example: “GREEN” in green → press. “GREEN” in blue → don’t press.
• Scores depend on reaction speed, fake-out control and mistakes.
Each difficulty tweaks: number of colors, timing window, fake frequency and point penalties.
